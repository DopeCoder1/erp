Oracle ERP

What is Oracle ERP ?

Oracle ERP is an end-to-end solution for business management. Designed for businesses of all sizes, this cloud-hosted software includes best practices and built-in standards to support businesses in streamlining their processes. Oracle ERP is created with scalability and simplicity in mind to help automate key business processes, including planning and procurement, reporting, financials, project planning, product lifecycle, compliance and governance, and risk management among others. Oracle ERP is a completely integrated Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) that offers businesses a set of dependable apps to increase productivity, enhance control, and lower operating costs. It leverages a connected approach with the support of cloud technology to help companies build a better foundation in IT, engineering, telecommunication, sales, consulting services, financial services, marketing, and other services.

Oracle ERP features

  • Financial planning and analysis, Financial close
  • Accounting, Enterprise performance management
  • Revenue recognition, Risk management
  • Inventory and supply chain, Procurement
  • Financial close, Project planning and execution
